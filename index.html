AI is everywhere.
Governance isn't.

Organisations are adopting AI tools faster than they can govern them. The EU AI Act is now in force, ISO 42001 sets the bar for AI management systems, and regulators worldwide are demanding documented evidence of AI governance. Without a visibility and control layer, every AI interaction is an untracked risk.

How It Works

Four steps from invisible risk to full control

Svalin operates at the machine level — capturing data flows before they leave the device, without routing through your network.

1

See everything — including what bypasses the network

Svalin's agent captures every AI tool data transfer at the machine level, including flows that bypass API gateways and network proxies entirely.

2

Set the rules, enforce them automatically

Define which data categories can flow through which AI tool connections. The policy engine warns or blocks in real time — without interrupting your team's workflow.

3

Prove compliance when asked

Export structured audit reports for GDPR, EU AI Act, SOC 2, ISO 27001, ISO 42001, or NIST AI RMF. Every governance decision is logged with full context.

4

Zero disruption for your team

The local agent never interferes with AI tool calls. Employees keep working at full speed. If Svalin goes down, nothing changes for end users.
