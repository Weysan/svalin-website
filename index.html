Why Now

AI is everywhere.
Governance isn't.

Organisations are adopting AI tools faster than they can govern them. The EU AI Act is now in effect, ISO 42001 sets the bar for AI management systems, and regulators worldwide are demanding transparency. Without a governance layer, every AI interaction is an untracked risk.

No visibility

Most organisations have no idea what data flows to AI models or what tools are being used.

Regulatory pressure

The EU AI Act, SOC 2, and ISO 42001 require documented AI risk management and audit trails.

Security blind spots

AI tools with access to sensitive data operate without the same controls as traditional software.

Features

Everything you need to govern AI

From audit trails to policy enforcement, Svalin gives security and compliance teams full control over AI usage.

Audit Trail

Complete, tamper-evident record of every data flow to and from AI models. Know exactly what was sent, when, and by whom — ready for auditors.

Observability Tools

Real-time monitoring of AI tool usage across your organisation. See which models are accessed, which tools are invoked, and what data is exchanged.

Policy Engine

Define and enforce rules governing how AI tools are used. Set data-flow policies, restrict tool access, and ensure compliance with organisational standards.
